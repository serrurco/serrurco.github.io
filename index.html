About the Firm

We invest where our work gives us conviction.

Serrur & Co. is a New York-based investment management firm. We pursue opportunities across asset classes and across the capital structure - in equity, credit, real estate, and private investments - with a consistent emphasis on fundamental value and the protection of capital.

Our work frequently leads us to complex and event-driven situations - restructurings, litigation, and dislocated markets - where analytical depth and patience create an advantage. We are long-term, concentrated, and independent in our approach.

Investment Approach
01

Fundamental Value

We seek to invest in businesses and assets at prices well below our assessment of intrinsic value. Our holding period is defined by the investment thesis - not by the calendar or the market.

02

Capital Preservation

Risk management is not a separate function. It is embedded in how we size positions, structure exposure, and select the point in the capital structure that best protects against permanent loss.

03

Analytical Depth

We invest where rigorous legal, financial, and procedural analysis - applied to situations others find too complex or too opaque - provides a margin of safety that the broader market has not recognized.
